The First LP

Jinx is where the 'Crumb sound' became a 'Crumb world.' It’s a 27-minute trip through hazy psych-rock and unsettling jazz. It was recorded in various studios across New York, marking their transition into a full-time touring band.

Track Highlights

Ghostride

"You're just a ghost in the passenger seat..." — This track perfectly captures the feeling of drifting through life while disconnected from reality.

Nina

One of the band's tightest grooves. The music video features a strange, stalker-like figure, leaning into the album's theme of paranoia.

Part III

Widely considered the emotional core of the album. It starts as a quiet ballad and explodes into a heavy, swirling psychedelic climax.
