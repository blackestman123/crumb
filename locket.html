The Breakthrough

Released just a year after their debut, Locket showcased a band that had found its confidence. The songwriting is tighter, the production is deeper, and the 'melting' sound is fully realized.

The Album

Locket

Track List

01. Plants

"Counting the days, checking the time..." - A song about the slow passage of time and the feeling of being stuck.

02. Recently Played

A smoky, saxophone-heavy track that feels like a modern noir film.

03. Locket

The crown jewel. A song about the weight of memories and the feeling of being trapped inside your own head.

04. Thirty-Nine

A hypnotic closer that uses repetitive loops to create a trance-like state.
