The "Patchwork" Experiment

AMAMA is Crumb at their most animated. It uses phone recordings, nautical blips, and piano strings dampened with Silly Putty. It’s a record about home—both the one you’re born into and the one you build on the road.

Key Tracks

"AMAMA"

The emotional core. Built around a digital sample of Lila's grandmother, blending family history with psychedelic textures.

"Crushxd"

A fast-paced 'road song' with a tragic back-story. It shows the band's ability to turn a random tour moment into a psych-pop gem.

"The Bug"

A frantic, rhythmic track that dates back to the band's early days. It captures the nervous energy of life on the road.
